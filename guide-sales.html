1. Creating a New Order

Supervisor
1
Click "+ New Order" button
Found at the bottom of the screen on all tabs.
2
Select Customer
Click the customer box — a dropdown shows all customers. Type to filter. If customer doesn't exist, click "+ Add New Customer" to create one.
3
Set Order & Delivery Date
Click the date boxes — a calendar pops up. Select the order date and delivery date. Set delivery time if needed.
4
Choose Fulfillment & Doc Type
Fulfillment: Delivery (driver sends) or Collection (customer picks up).
Doc Type: Cash Sales (CS) for cash customers, Delivery Order (DO) for credit customers. Auto-set based on customer payment terms.
5
Add Products
Click "+ Add Item". Select product, enter quantity, price, and ripeness index (if applicable). Add multiple items as needed.
6
Create Order
Review totals and click "Create Order". The order appears in the "Order Received" section.

2. Start Preparing

Supervisor
1
Click into the order → "Start Preparing"
From Orders tab, click the order card, then click "Start Preparing".
2
Assign a Worker
Select a worker from the dropdown. This shows who is responsible for preparing the order.
3
Send WhatsApp to Worker
After selecting a worker, a WhatsApp message preview appears in Bahasa Malaysia with order details. Click "WhatsApp" to send directly, or "Copy" to paste into a group chat.
4
Confirm & Start
Click "Confirm & Start" to move the order to "Preparing" status.
Tip: The assigned worker's name appears on the order card and in the Dashboard's Active Orders table.

3. Mark as Prepared

Worker / Supervisor
1
Click "Mark Prepared"
Open the order in "Preparing" status and click "Mark Prepared".
2
Confirm Actual Quantities
A popup shows all ordered items with editable quantities. Adjust if the actual prepared amount differs from the order (e.g., ordered 500kg but only 480kg available). Totals recalculate automatically.
3
Take Photo (Optional)
Choose "Take Photo" (camera) or "From Album" (gallery) to attach a preparation photo. Or click "Skip" to proceed without a photo.
Tip: Photos can be added later from the order detail page under the "Photos" section.

4. Assign Driver & Deliver

Supervisor
1
For Delivery orders: "Assign Driver & Deliver"
Select a driver from the dropdown and confirm. Order moves to "Ready For Delivery".
2
For Collection orders: "Mark Collected"
If the customer picks up, click "Mark Collected". Order goes straight to "Completed".

5. Complete Delivery

Driver Supervisor
1
Click "Mark Delivered"
From the order detail page when status is "Ready For Delivery".
2
Take Delivery Photo (Optional)
Take a photo or choose from album as proof of delivery.
3
Collect Payment (Cash Sales only)
For Cash Sales orders, a payment popup appears with the balance pre-filled. Enter amount collected, select method (Cash/Bank Transfer). Click "Confirm Payment & Print" or "Skip — Print Receipt" if unpaid.
Delivery Orders (DO) skip this step — DOs are paid via QB Invoice.
4
Receipt Generated
An 80mm receipt pops up showing order details and payment status (PAID/UNPAID). Print it or share via WhatsApp.

6. Recording Payments (After Delivery)

Supervisor
1
Go to Payments tab
Shows all completed Cash Sales orders grouped by customer. Only completed orders appear here.
2
Expand a customer → Pay individual or batch
Click a customer row to expand. Use checkboxes to select multiple orders, then click "Pay Selected" for batch payment. Or click "Pay" on a single order.
3
Enter payment details
Amount (pre-filled), method (Cash/Bank Transfer/Cheque), reference number. Optionally attach a bank transfer slip photo.
Note: Delivery Orders do NOT appear in Payments — they are handled through Invoicing tab (QB Invoice).

7. Invoicing Delivery Orders

Supervisor
1
Go to Invoicing tab
Shows uninvoiced DOs grouped by customer.
2
Select DOs to batch into an invoice
Expand a customer, tick the DOs to include. A Billing Summary appears showing aggregated products, quantities, and totals.
3
Copy or Print the Billing Summary
Use "Copy" to paste into QuickBooks, or "Print" for a paper copy to hand to billing staff.
4
Mark as Invoiced
Click "Mark as Invoiced", enter the QB Invoice Number and date, then save. DOs move to Invoice History.

Quick Reference

Dashboard

Your daily overview — active orders, upcoming deliveries, outstanding payments, uninvoiced DOs.

Document Types

Cash Sales (CS)Cash customers. Pay immediately or after delivery. Payment tracked in Payments tab.
Delivery Order (DO)Credit customers. Batched into QB invoices. No direct payment — handled through Invoicing tab.

Key Rules

  • Orders can only be edited when in "Order Received" status
  • Payments can only be recorded on completed orders
  • Quantities can be adjusted when marking as Prepared (actual vs ordered)
  • Photos can be added at any time from the order detail page
  • WhatsApp messages are only generated when a worker is assigned
